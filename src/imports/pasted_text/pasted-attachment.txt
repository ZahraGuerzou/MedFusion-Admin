Weight Selection
In our experiments, we set wssl = 0.5, wacc = 0.3, and wsize = 0.2. These values reflect
the design priorities of our framework, where SSL quality is given the highest importance as
it directly measures representation usefulness, while accuracy is assigned a moderate weight
to preserve predictive performance, and dataset size is included with a smaller contribution
to ensure statistical reliability without dominating the aggregation. Overall, this weighting
strategy emphasizes representation learning while maintaining a balance between performance
and stability.
